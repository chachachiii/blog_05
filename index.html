Powercast’s Ubiquity uses RF to charge devices wirelessly


https://venturebeat.com/games/powercasts-ubiquity-uses-rf-to-charge-devices-wirelessly/
January 3, 2023

Powercast's latest invention Ubiquity is trying to improve the way we charge our devices wirelessly. They are using radio frequency technology to charge low power devices. By using a transmitter, it converts the radio frequencies into DC, essentially charging your low-powered devices safely from a distance. Depending how low-powered your device is, the further away you can charge from the transmitter, with normal low-powered devices charging from within several feet, and ultra low-powered devices charging from up to 120 feet away.
